Hercules is a Bank’s strategic account platform runs on on-prem environment. A third-party CRM system Venus is upstream to Hercules. The data from Hercules is then loaded into Atlas – a data integration platform that loads into enterprise targets systems - Jupiter, Titan, and Achilles. Jupiter is the regulatory Fin Crime system. Pegasus is a transaction monitoring system that consumes data from Jupiter for bank’s alert monitoring system  Pyrrhus.
